Privacy Policy

UCircuit respects your privacy. The app is designed to be a safe learning experience and does not require accounts or logins.

Data Collection

UCircuit does not collect, store, transmit, or sell personal information.

  • No user accounts
  • No advertising
  • No tracking
  • No third-party analytics SDKs

In-App Purchases

UCircuit offers a one-time in-app purchase to unlock additional learning content. Payments are processed by Apple. UCircuit does not receive your payment details.

Children’s Privacy

UCircuit is designed for learning and is suitable for use with parental guidance. The app does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes

If this policy changes, updates will be posted on this page.
